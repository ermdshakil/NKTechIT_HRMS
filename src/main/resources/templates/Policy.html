📘 NKTechIT - Company Policy

1. Work Hours

Employees are expected to work from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Remote work options may be available based on roles and responsibilities.

2. Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 12 annual paid leaves, 10 casual/sick leaves, and all national holidays as per the Indian government calendar.

3. Code of Conduct

All employees must maintain professional behavior, avoid conflicts of interest, and comply with data privacy and security protocols.

4. Security Policy

NKTechIT follows strict cybersecurity practices. All systems should be accessed securely, and unauthorized sharing of credentials is prohibited.

5. Dress Code

Employees are expected to wear smart casuals. Formal attire is recommended during client meetings or presentations.

6. Internet & Email Use

Internet and email should be used responsibly for work-related purposes. Personal use should be limited during office hours.
